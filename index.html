Paris Fashion Week

Paris:The home of Fashion

Paris is considered to be the fashion capital. It is considered as leadership role in the world of Fashion,Style and the emergence of new trends. In addition, fashion capitals have a broad mix of business, artistic, entertainment, cultural, and leisure activities that are internationally recognized for having a unique and strong identity.The status of a fashion capital is also linked to the city's domestic and international profile. Fashion capitals are part of a wider social construct scene , with design schools, fashion magazines, and powerful market of affluent consumers of fashion.
